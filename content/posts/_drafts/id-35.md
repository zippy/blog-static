One of the specters that frequently appears when discussing community currency is the ideas that people have about "human nature." Invariably what people are referring to when they make this refference is to the mean and selfish parts of human nature. Of course human nature also has extremely selfless and generous aspects as well. So why is it that when we talk about the economic (and especially monetary) sphere, that generosity and cooperativeness is not what comes to mind when we utter the words "human nature" in that context?
